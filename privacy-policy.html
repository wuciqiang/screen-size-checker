Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Screen Size Checker. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you visit our website and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

2. Information We Collect

We collect and process the following information:

  • Device information (screen resolution, viewport size, DPR)
  • Browser information (browser type, version)
  • Operating system information
  • User agent string
  • Language preferences
  • Cookie preferences

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our screen size checking service
  • Analyze and enhance website performance
  • Remember your language preferences
  • Improve user experience

4. Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your language preferences
  • Analyze website usage
  • Improve our service

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect some website functionality.

5. Data Storage

All information is processed locally in your browser. We do not store any personal information on our servers.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Request restriction of processing your data
