1. Introduction

Welcome to Screen Size Checker. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your information. This page explains what is processed when you use our website.

2. Information We Process

When you use the tools on this website, the following technical information may be processed in your browser:

  • Screen resolution and viewport dimensions
  • Device pixel ratio (DPR)
  • Browser and operating system information
  • User agent string
  • Language and cookie preferences

3. How Information Is Used

We use this information to operate and improve the service, including:

  • Providing screen-size and viewport tools
  • Improving feature quality and reliability
  • Remembering language and display preferences
  • Understanding general usage patterns

4. Cookies

Cookies may be used for language selection, basic preferences, and anonymous analytics. You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

5. Data Storage

Core measurements are computed locally in your browser. We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information through these tools.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of personal information. Contact us to submit a request.
